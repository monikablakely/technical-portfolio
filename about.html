About Me

Hi, I’m Monika!

I’m actively seeking entry-level IT roles such as IT Specialist, Help Desk, Desktop Support, and I’m open to exploring other opportunities where I can grow and contribute. My long-term goal is to become an IT project manager, and I’m currently focused on strengthening my technical foundation beyond what I’ve gained through education and project work.

Growing up, my family moved every three years because my dad served in the Navy. I’ve lived in places like Bahrain and Japan, which gave me a broader perspective and a love for experiencing different cultures. I’m Black and Japanese and attended a Japanese school on weekends, so I’m fluent in Japanese.

In my free time, I stay active with running, enjoy cooking, and love going to concerts because live music is one of my favorite things.
